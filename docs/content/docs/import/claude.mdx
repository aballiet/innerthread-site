## What you need

A Claude data export (ZIP file containing `conversations.json`).

## How to export from Claude

1. Go to [claude.ai](https://claude.ai)
2. Click your profile icon in the bottom-left corner
3. Click **Settings**
4. Go to **Privacy & Data**
5. Click **Export data**
6. Wait for the email from Anthropic (usually within a few minutes)
7. Click the download link in the email (expires after 24 hours)
8. Unzip the archive on your Mac

Your conversations are in `conversations.json`.

## How to import into Retrace

1. Open Retrace and go to **Settings**
2. Click the **Data Sources** tab
3. Click the **Claude** card
4. Drop your `conversations.json` file or click to select it
5. Review the conversations found in the preview
6. Click **Import**

## What gets imported

- All conversations with their names
- Every message (both your prompts and Claude's responses)
- Timestamps and message ordering

## Good to know

- **No participant linking needed**: Claude conversations are between you and the assistant. Retrace assigns them automatically.
- **Export link expires**: The download link expires after 24 hours. Download it promptly.
- **Re-importing**: Safe to do. Retrace deduplicates messages automatically.

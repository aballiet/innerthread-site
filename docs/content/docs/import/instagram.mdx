title: Import Instagram DMs
description: How to export and import your Instagram direct messages into Retrace.

## What you need

An Instagram data export containing your messages (JSON format).

## How to export from Instagram

1. Open Instagram and go to **Settings and activity**
2. Tap **Accounts Center** (or go to [accountscenter.instagram.com](https://accountscenter.instagram.com))
3. Tap **Your information and permissions**
4. Tap **Download your information**
5. Select your Instagram account
6. Choose **Select types of information** and check only **Messages**
7. Set the format to **JSON**
8. Click **Submit request**
9. Wait for the email from Meta (can take up to 48 hours)
10. Download the archive and unzip it on your Mac

Your DMs are in the `messages/inbox/` folder inside the extracted archive.

## How to import into Retrace

1. Open Retrace and go to **Settings**
2. Click the **Data Sources** tab
3. Click the **Instagram** card
4. Select the `inbox` folder from your extracted archive
5. Review the conversations found in the preview
6. Link participants to people in your address book (or create new ones)
7. Click **Import**

## What gets imported

- All direct messages with timestamps and sender names
- Group chat participants
- Message metadata (reactions, shares, replies)

## Common issues

- **Export takes a long time**: Instagram exports can take up to 48 hours. You'll get an email when the archive is ready.
- **Same format as Messenger**: Instagram and Messenger exports use the same JSON structure (both are Meta products). The parser handles both.
- **Re-importing**: Safe to do. Retrace deduplicates messages automatically.

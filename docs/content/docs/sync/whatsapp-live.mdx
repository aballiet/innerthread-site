## What it does

WhatsApp Live Sync connects to WhatsApp Web and syncs your messages automatically. New messages appear in Retrace as they arrive. No more exporting `.txt` files.

## Set up live sync

1. Open Retrace and go to **Settings**
2. Click the **Data Sources** tab
3. Click **WhatsApp Live Sync**
4. A QR code will appear
5. Open WhatsApp on your phone
6. Go to **Linked Devices** and tap **Link a Device**
7. Scan the QR code with your phone

Once linked, Retrace syncs messages in real-time.

## What gets synced

- New incoming and outgoing messages across all chats
- Group messages
- Message metadata (timestamps, sender, read status)

## Good to know

- **Keeps existing WhatsApp Web working**: Retrace acts as an additional linked device. Your WhatsApp Web session is unaffected.
- **Phone must stay online**: WhatsApp requires your phone to have an internet connection for linked devices to work.
- **Initial sync**: On first connection, Retrace syncs recent message history. After that, it captures messages in real-time.
- **Reconnection**: If Retrace is closed and reopened, it reconnects automatically and catches up on missed messages.

## Privacy

- Messages are synced directly from WhatsApp's servers to your machine
- No data passes through any third-party service
- The connection uses WhatsApp's end-to-end encryption
